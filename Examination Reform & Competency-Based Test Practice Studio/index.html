Mock Exam Simulator

🎯 Full-Length Board Pattern Exam

Simulates a complete exam with all sections: MCQ, Case-Based, Short Answer, and Long Answer. Timed sections with section-wise analysis.

Exam Structure

  • Section A: 20 MCQs (1 mark each) – 20 min
  • Section B: 2 Case-based passages (4 marks each) – 15 min
  • Section C: 5 Short answers (3 marks each) – 20 min
  • Section D: 3 Long answers (5 marks each) – 25 min
  • Total: 60 marks | 80 minutes
